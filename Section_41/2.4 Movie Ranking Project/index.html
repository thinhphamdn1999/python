Top Movies of All Time According to Thinh

My best to 3 movies

Sprited Away

It is a Japanese animated fantasy film written and directed by Hayao Miyazaki. It was released in 2001 and won the Academy Award for Best Animated Feature in 2003

Interstellar

It is a science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. It was released in 2014 and stars Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hathaway, and Jessica Chastain.

Parasite

It is a South Korean black comedy thriller film directed by Bong Joon-ho. It was released in 2019 and won the Academy Award for Best Picture in 2020.
